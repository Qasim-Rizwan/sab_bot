üêõ COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Issue: "Python is not recognized"
Solution: Reinstall Python and check "Add Python to PATH" during installation

Issue: "Node is not recognized"
Solution: Reinstall Node.js and restart your computer

Issue: "Port already in use"
Solution: Close other applications or restart your computer

Issue: Page doesn't load
Solution: Wait 15-20 seconds after starting, then refresh browser

Issue: Chatbot shows errors
Solution: Check backend window for error messages, restart backend.bat
